The Allure of Paper  
     
Organized in celebration of the Amon Carter's 50th Anniversary, this special exhibition showcases more than 100 drawings and watercolors from the museum's holdings.

These one-of-a-kind works of art range in date from the early 19th century to the late 20th century and chronicle the sweeping changes that occurred in American art.

Tuesday, Wednesday, Friday, Saturday:
10 a.m.-5 p.m.
Thursday: 10 a.m.-8 p.m.
Sunday: Noon-5 p.m.
Closed Mondays and major holidays.

Fort Worth, TX 76107-2695

Picasso and Braque  
     
You might to go on over to the Kimball in Dallas to see Picasso and Braque: The Cubist Experiment, 1910-1912. This small exhibit of paintings and etchings highlights this exuberant period of art puzzles that suggest three dimensions.

Kimbell Art Museum

Fort Worth, Texas 76107-2792

More Grove Wine Bar  
     
Did you see that the Grove on Bee Caves Road will open a "to go" location next door at the end of the summer? It will include onsite meals and a delivery service for addresses within five miles of the restaurant. Currently they are not available Thurs - Sat because of the high volume of customers at the Grove. By the way, I saw this in Community Impact Newspaper, Lake Travis and Westlake Edition,a newspaper that focuses on local businesses and what they're doing. I get it free by mail.
