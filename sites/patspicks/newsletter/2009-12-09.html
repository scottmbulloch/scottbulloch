Great gifts under $25  
     

It can be hard to find quality gifts and not spend a fortune. Here are some suggestions for smaller gifts that show you care:

Christmas-scented candles in glass with a lovely silver cross. $13
Set of two embossed soaps in a beautiful boxes. $16
"Homey" Scented candles in a square jar from Comfort, Texas. I loved the "comfort" scent.
All are from Northwest Hills Pharmacy (Ask for Toni!) And they gift wrap at no charge.

If you haven't tried Peppermint Hot Chocolate, you must! (Also available without the peppermint) $19.50 at Williams-Sonoma.

Beautiful cranberry ball candle on a clear glass coaster. $18 for both
OK, this is more than $20 but a great gift for a wine lover who can't wait! A red wine aerator that will put just the right amount of air in the wine so you don't need to wait for it to breathe. $40
Both at Crate and Barrel

Sure picking out a book for someone else is tricky, but Amazon's list of the top books of 2009 may give you some ideas.

Measuring spoons in Victorian patterns…pretty & functional! $24 at www.anthropologie.com

Dessert plates that express your excuse for you! "Oh no, I couldn't", "OK. Just a sliver." Boxed in a mini hatbox. $15 at Target.

Antique silver (plated) spreaders from Pottery Barn. They look vintage…$19 and free shipping from the Web site.

Tuck three or four jars of preserves from Fredericksburg Farms in a basket. I especially like Homemade Peach Preserves. Yum! Available at Central Market. $6 each
