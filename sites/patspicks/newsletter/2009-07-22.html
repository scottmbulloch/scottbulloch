Do I really need a reason to love chocolate?  
     

I love chocolate… I really love chocolate. Now there are even more reasons to eat it. Dr. Joseph Maroon, author of The Longevity Factor, says just 30 calories a day of dark (70 percent or more cocoa) is enough to help keep blood pressure down. Serving should be about the size of a Hershey kiss. More reasons to eat chocolate. (Do we really need more?)


    
Food Tidbits  
     

My friend Joyce Moeller eats lots of kiwi fruit… now I know why! Yes, they are tasty, but they also have about 70 milligrams of vitamin C (more than an orange) in each fruit. Vitamin C is linked to improved eyesight, lower cancer risks, and better heart health. The kiwi also has 20 percent more potassium than bananas and is one of the few fruits that contains vitamin E. From Health magazine

If you eat the same thing every day for one meal, you are more likely to lose weight. Why? Drs. Oz and Roizen say it's because having more choices may make you eat like you're in a speed eating contest. You know how you like to try everything at a big buffet! Fewer choices, less added weight.
