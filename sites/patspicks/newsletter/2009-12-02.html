From the International Desk! (Jen Luin Meadows)  
     

Konnichiwa! Greetings from Japan! It's been an adventure just to get here, but I've been in country for over a week now, and am starting to get settled in. Saw this as I was perusing the web this morning: Lucky magazine has put together a slide show of a few selected pieces from some of the diffusion lines, that is, the pairing of mass retailers with designers.

Target of course has been one of the most prolific, pairing with a new designer nearly every quarter to offer a new line. However, these lines are now popping up everywhere from JCPenney to Anthropologie. You almost have to see the lines to know if they're OK. Some come off really well and others just don't have the quality to be a good deal (at any price).

Speaking of deals, check my blog for my shopping adventure at Ann Taylor.
