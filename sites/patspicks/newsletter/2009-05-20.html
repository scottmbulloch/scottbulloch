Texas Masters of Fine Art and Craft Invitational Exhibit and Sale  
     
I know it's really hard to get away at this time of year with lots of activities winding down, but if you can head out this weekend (My colleagues at the Capitol are laughing hysterically now), go to Kerrville.

The Texas Masters of Fine Art and Craft Invitational Exhibit and Sale will showcase some of Texas' finest jewelry, sculpture, paintings and pottery. Hosted by Dan & Linda Baker, extraordinary jewelers from Dallas. All at the Y.O. Ranch Resort, 2033 Sydney Baker (Hwy 16), Kerrville

May 22-24
Friday 12pm - 8pm
Saturday 10am - 8pm
Sunday 10am - 6pm
Free Admission

    
A Little of Everything  
     
And for something unusual, head to Gibson's store... EVERYTHING packed into that store... what a great place! From cast iron cookware, to 10 different kinds of hummingbird feeders, all types of kitchen "necessities" to clothes, hats/boots, full hardware, automotive, decorative pots and lawn ornaments, fountains and lawn decorative items, sewing items, food, beverages. WHAT A KICK! Lou Ann Caywood & her husband Gary spent 45 minutes there and did not see everything. 111 W. Main, Kerrville

Lou Ann also says one of the very best quilt shops in South Texas is Creations... they are building a retreat center next to the store. 1013 E. Main, Kerrville

And next weekend (May 22-24) the Hill Country Quilt Guild will hold their Quilt Show at Schreiner Institute. More info at the Creations Website above.

And after all that shopping, try La Margarita for a fun place to eat. 2145 Sidney Baker St

And if that weren't enough, the 38th Annual Texas Arts & Crafts Fair will be held the same weekend.

Saturday, May 23rd - 10am - 6pm
Sunday, May 24th - 10am - 6pm
Monday, May 25th - 10am - 4pm
