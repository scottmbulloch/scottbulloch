Books for Cancer Support  
     
I have found these books helpful in dealing with cancer. With 1 out of 3 women and 1 out of 2 men expected to have cancer at some time during their lives, you probably know someone who is facing it. I found used copies on Amazon.

Anti-Cancer by Dr. David Servan-Schrieber. It describes the lifestyle and nutritional changes that Dr. Schrieber made when he was diagnosed with brain cancer. He is a 15-year survivor.

From Incurable to Incredible by Tami Boehmer. True stories of people who did not accept their pessimistic prognoses and created healing. Very uplifting.
