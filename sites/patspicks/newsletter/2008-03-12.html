Gallery Night at West Galleries  
     
Every Thursday Night the 8 galleries of the West End open their doors for a gallery crawl. All galleries are in walking distance in the 6th Street area. View a list of galleries and their current exhibitions.

By the way, Wally Workman Gallery has the most beautiful showing of Gordon Fowler paintings. If you love the impressionists, you must see this Texas artist.

Paintings Texas to France March 1 - 29
Traveling with paints is my way of getting to know a culture, at least visually. France, especially Provence, is to me an exciting place to paint due to the great artists who made their mark there. As I painted in the old villages and fields of Van Gogh, Cezanne, Matisse, Picasso and countless others, I had the most humble feelings about being in these sacred art spaces. I knew somehow they were watching me, invading their special pleinair spots, and I tried to paint with the passion they must have had.
- Gordon Fowler

Margaret's Musings  
     
The super-duper Texas primary is over, and a lot of folks are still super confused about the Democratic party's super delegates.
