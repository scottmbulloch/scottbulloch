Watermelons say it's still summer!  
     

Watermelons are one of three fruits (the others are cherries and nectarines) that have compounds that may boost your skin's natural resilience against wrinkles and sun damage, according to Allison Tannis, author of Feed Your Skin, Starve Your Wrinkles. RealAge.com recommends leaving the melon on the counter until you are ready to slice it because it will continue to build up antioxidants at room temp.Of course, refrigerate after slicing.

Target News!  
     

As you know, my favorite designer discount Web site is Gilt.com. Gilt and Target are partnering to offer the new Target-designer collaborations to Gilt customers on August 20 for 36 hours before they become available at Target. This blog shows some of the items.

I am particularly interested in the Temple St. Clair for Target line, which debuts online on August 29. I love the designer's original line but pieces start at $1000 and are beyond my budget. I'm hoping the Target line looks good but, as usual with these designer collaborations with Target, you have to see them in person to check the quality.
