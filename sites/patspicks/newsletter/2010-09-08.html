Food and Wine Events  
     

Jan Hesselsweet says there are some fun food and wine events coming up! First, there is the Dripping with Taste Wine and Food Festival on September 11 in (you guessed it) Dripping Springs at the Creekside Pavillion. The Pavillion is across from the Salt Lick and directions are on the Web site.

Art and food vendors and 17 wineries will be there noon to 7pm. Wineries include Becker Vineyards, Fall Creek, Texas Hills, Messina Hof and lots more.
$25 ($30 at the gate)

and more…

On October 8-10, the 6th Annual Texas Fall & Wine Fest will be held in Marble Falls with the best wine and food in the Hill Country! Everything from a progressive dinner to an outdoor fair. These are the same folks who started the Texas Hill Country Wine & Food Festival in Austin. We'll give you a reminder as we get closer to the event.


    
More Texas Olive Oil  
     

Some time ago we told you about Bella Vista Ranch, an olive orchard in Wimberley, that offers tours of the facility.

There is another olive ranch in South Texas. Texas Olive Ranch Harvest & Gold Crush will be open to visitors September 14-15 to observe the largest olive harvest in Texas history from 9am to 2pm. BUT the ranch is in Carrizo Springs so this may be of more interest to our San Antonio readers. It is about 2 hours southwest of San Antonio. Or take a trip to Whole Foods or Central Market and check out their products! Meyer Lemon Olive Oil or Fig-infused Balsamic Vinegar…yum!


    
How about a Cattle Drive!  
     

If your kiddos or grandchildren haven't seen Longhorn cattle any closer than the upper deck of Memorial Stadium, you need to get to Georgetown on Friday or Saturday (September 17 & 18) for the Up the Chisholm Trail Cattle Drive & Chuckwagon Cook-off. The cook-off is Friday and the cattle drive is Saturday afternoon. Yee-ha!

Do-Over Retreat

If you have a runner in the family  
     

Joyce and Dick Moeller tell us that the Water to Thrive 5K and Kids K will be held at Hill Country Galleria on Saturday, September 18. We're talking a band, singers, kids' activities! The event starts at 8:30am and ends at 10am… in time to hit the stores! If you remember, Water to Thrive is the nonprofit that travels to Ethiopia to put in wells; they have brought clean water to over 75,000 Ethiopians. Register here.
