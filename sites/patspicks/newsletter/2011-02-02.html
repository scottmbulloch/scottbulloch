Paper Source  
     
If you want to get in the mood for Valentine's Day stop by the new Austin location of Paper Source,a shop full of unique wrappings and paper goods. Right now it looks like a pink and red Valentine! The grand opening is Friday, February 4. It is located at the corner of 6th and Lamar in the shopping center with Chico's.

Boden now has maternity  
     
I've mentioned Boden before for colorful knitwear for men, women, and children. Now they have a (limited) selection of maternity clothes.

    
In the Garden at the Westbank Library  
     
Lots of interesting events for children, adults, and the whole family. At both the Westbank and Laura's Library locations. Everything from stargazing and sustainable gardening to a Valentine sunset with appetizers and wine! The Web site has the entire list.

    
Vitamin D update  
     
Remember I mentioned that it may be harder to get enough vitamin D from the sun than I thought. Well, I had mine checked for the first time...I have a severe deficiency! So I'm on supplements now.
