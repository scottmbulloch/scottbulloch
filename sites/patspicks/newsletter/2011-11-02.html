Unique Christmas cards  
     
Artist Nancy Paton has designed two new cards... both are handpainted, a Christmas agave and a Christmas frog. The cards are $20 for a box of 10. The cards are 4.5 by 6.25 inches. Both designs are the same size. They may be purchased at these venues.
Nov 5, Wesley gallery show
Nov 16, Dripping Springs Womens Club Holiday Marketplace
You might also contact Nancy at paton_nancy@hotmail.com if you want some.

By the way, that Nov. 5 date is the Fine Arts Festival at Wesley Gallery at 27008 Ranch Road 12 (half mile south of Hwy. 290 W) in Dripping Springs. 10am - 5pm.
For a map to the gallery
888-806-0678

    
Don't wait for an emergency  
     
The recent Bastrop fires reminds us that we need to know what documents need to be available and how to organize them. Amy Pratt of On the Record Organizing will offer a seminar this Sat to show you what you to do to prepare for emergencies. It includes a notebook for the types of documents you will need. Click here for more info and to register.
This is offered only once a year!
$95
One Highland Center
10 - noon, Nov 5 (register by Nov 3)

Twenty + Texas Hill Country wineries will offer a tasting of their best wines. A third party will evaluate wines for quality and consistency. $50 ($55 at the door) at the Paramount Theatre
6 - 9:30 (tasting ends at 9)

    
East Austin Studio Tour  
     
The tour doesn't start until Nov 12 (thru Nov 20) but I'm mentioning it now because the catalogues are available now at all 22 Austin Public Libraries. The tour is so big now that it really helps to have the catalog and they do run out. This show is fascinating... there is everything from the established artists to some weird stuff. You can't see it all, even in two weekends!
