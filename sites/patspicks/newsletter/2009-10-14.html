Easy Country Fried Quail

  • 4 semi boneless quail, cut in quarters
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • ¼ - ½ cups Louisiana Hot Sauce
  • 2 cups flour
  • salt & pepper
  • oil for deep frying

Preheat oil to 350 degrees. Season the flour with salt & pepper and set aside. Combine buttermilk & hot sauce & marinate quail for 30 minutes. Dredge the quail, one piece ata time, in seasoned flour, pressing lightly with your fingertips to ensure that it is coated. Place quail in oil & fry until golden brown & crunchy. Serve as an appetizer with Jalapeno Ranch or Country style gravy.

Jalapeno ranch

  • ½ jalapeno
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 cup ranch dressing

Sautee jalapeno, whole garlic & green onion over high heat until garlic is cooked through & peppers & onions are browned on the edges. Puree mixture and add to ranch dressing.
