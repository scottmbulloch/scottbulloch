Summer Sangria  
     
I haven't tried this but the Texas Hill Country wineries sent this out and it sounds yummy!

Stone Fruit Sangria
In a pitcher or large bowl, combine 1 peach, 1 nectarine and 1 plum, each halved, pitted and sliced into 1/2 inch wedges, 16 cherries, pitted, 3 tablespoons peach liqueur and 1 bottle of your favorite Hill Country dry white wine. Stir to combine and refrigerate 1 hour (or up to overnight). To serve, add 4 cups chilled ginger ale and ice.
