2009 Pond Tour  
     

July 18 & 19
Sat 9-5, Sat 8-11pm, Sun 9-5
$15, $12.50 in advance on the Web site

Over 30 homes will be open to showcase some of the most interesting ponds in Central Texas. It's almost impossible to see them all, so research them before you head out. Some you might not want to miss include:

  • Saturday, Pond #5 & #6 at 2304 & 2400 Waterwell Lane. Small (but beautiful) backyard ponds that would fit in most yards.
  • Saturday nite #1 at 1103 Ridgecrest Dr. Elaborate five-pond installation
  • Sunday #1 at 3705 Corum Cove. Huge 40,000 gallon pond and maze.
  • Sunday #3 at 410 Skyline Dr. Features a waterfall, stream, & bog
  • Sunday #4 at 406 Skyline Dr. Unusual front yard pond with goldfish & lilies

Eleanor Powell has been doing the tour for years and says the pond on Corum Cove is not to be missed! The Web site is a little tricky to navigate; make sure you start here.
