Change of seasons at the farmer's market  
     
The produce at the farmers markets will be changing now. It may over 100 degrees but the produce will be saying fall. I've mentioned confituras, which are handmade jams and jellies that are sold at the Barton Creek market. Last weekend was the last time you could get pickled peaches and fig preserves. Look for things more like apple hatch chile chutney.
