It's Fall Foliage time at Lost Maples  
     

The area features the uncommon Uvalde Bigtooth Maple, whose fall foliage can be spectacular. Generally, the foliage changes the last two weeks of October through the first two weeks of November. The extreme heat this summer, however, has resulted in the trees changing colors early. Reports on the foliage will begin in October at the Web site.

3 Ways to prevent wrinkles  
     

Realage.com suggests:

Eat soy.
Early research suggests soy may help protect against - and possibly even heal some - photodamage caused by sun exposure.

Have a cup of cocoa.
Research shows that two of the antioxidants in cocoa - epicatechin and catechin - can help shield against sun damage and improve your skin's hydration and blood circulation.

Sleep on your back.
Sleeping with your face in the pillow every night can cause wrinkles known as "sleep lines," say folks from The American Academy of Dermatology.
