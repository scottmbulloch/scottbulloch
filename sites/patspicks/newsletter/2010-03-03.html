Bunkhouse Gallery's 18th Fine Art & Jewelry Show  
     

I just love the art shows of spring and here is one of the first and best in the Hill Country. These artists capture the colors and views of regional beauty in oils, pastels, and watercolors.

This is a working cattle ranch that has become popular as an artists' retreat because of its natural beauty.

A silent auction and original painting auction will benefit CASA for the Highland Lakes Area. Wine by McReynolds Winery and music both days.

March 6 & 7, 10am - 5pm, Cypress Mill, TX, at the Wenmohs Ranch, 573 Old Spicewood Road. For directions go to www.wenmohsranch.com

Rhubarb, a cancer killer?  
     

Alegria Hibbetts reports that Britain's Sheffield Hallam University says that rhubarb, especially when cooked, may be a factor in killing or preventing cancer cells. Rhubarb contains cancer--fighting polyphenols and baking for 20 minutes increases the concentration of polyphenols.
