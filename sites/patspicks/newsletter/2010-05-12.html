Martin + Osa closing  
     

There's good news and bad news. Bad news: All Martin + Osa stores nationwide will close in August. Good news: There are some great deals, especially on cardigans. 30% off everything but cardigans are already reduced to $39 so I paid $28 for beautiful cotton sweaters that retailed for $69! Further reductions will be taken in June but sizes may be a problem.
