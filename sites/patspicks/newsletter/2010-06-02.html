Belmont Breeze  
     

As you know by now, my daughter rides at Belmont Track on Long Island and next week is the Belmont Stakes (last leg of the triple crown). In honor of the occasion, here is the recipe for the official drink of the Stakes, the Belmont Breeze.

  • 1½ oz. Seagrams 7
  • ¾ oz. Harveys Bristol Cream Sherry
  • ½ oz. Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 1 oz. Simple Syrup
  • 1½ oz. Fresh Orange juice
  • 1½ oz. Cranberry juice
  • 1 oz. of soda
  • 1 oz. of 7up
  • fresh strawberry
  • lemon wedge
  • (you may substitute 1 oz. of Sweet & Sour mix for the lemon juice and simple syrup although fresh ingredients are best)

Preparation:
Shake first 6 ingredients with ice and top with half 7up and half soda, approximately one ounce of each. Garnish with fresh strawberry, a mint sprig, and a lemon wedge.
