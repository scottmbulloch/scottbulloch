Tax-free weekend August 21-23!  
     

Clothes and school supplies under $100 are tax-free. But many stores will run sales at the same time. Those are the best deals. Banana Republic, for example, is discounting new merchandise 25 percent on purchases of $100 or more with a Bananacard through August 23.


    
Online designer deals  
     

Sally Hanners mentioned that she has discovered Ideeli, an invitation-only Web site and e-letter that offers new discounts every week on designer goodies such as clothes, bags, jewelry, even sheets. I just ordered some Carolee lizard hoop earrings for $33 (retail $85).

Over a year ago, I mentioned a couple of these e-letters, but I thought I would mention them again for anyone who missed it. Gilt Groupe is probably the best known. They frequently feature clothes and shoes by Michael Kors, Vera Wang, and Zac Posen. Everything is at least half price, and the end of season sales have greater discounts. The big name designers such as Valentino and Carolina Herrera are still expensive (at least to me) but there are some very reasonable labels with dresses for $60-$140.

Rue La La offers designers such as DKNY, Cavalli, and Vera Wang Lavendar Label. I ordered a Michael Kors clutch for $88. Billion Dollar Babes was originally a traveling designer sample sale, but it now has an online presence. They tend to have a little bit edgier things, but I ordered a great pair of coin earrings.

The new kid on the block is Beyond the Rack. I haven't found anything yet that I just had to have, but it is new.

Word of warning: The shipping fees can eat you alive if you're not careful. Gilt.com, ideeli.com and RueLaLa.com have flat shipping fees of $10 and $11. I ordered a top that didn't fit and paid $10 to receive it and $10 to return it. They give credit, which meant I paid another $10 to ship the new item. BillionDollarBabes.com bases the fee on your location.

These are invitation-only so if you decide you want to see what it's all about just email me at info@patspicksaustin.com and I'll have them send you an invitation. There's no fee to sign up.
