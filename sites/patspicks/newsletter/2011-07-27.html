Grape Stomp is almost here  
     
The Texas wineries will offer a Harvest Wine Trail August 5-7 and August 12-14. Tickets are $60 per couple and include complimentary tastes of wine, a 15% discount on custom 3 bottle purchases, and a Kerrygold cheeses. There is also a photography contest that could win you a Wine Country Getaway!

Also check your favorite vineyard for their activities. Fall Creek, for example, has lots of food and wine harvest events that sound yummy! Luncheons featuring top chefs and wine selection instruction.

    
Enough sun!  
     
Physical sunscreens (titanium dioxide or zinc oxide) tend to be less irritating on the thin skin around the eye. Clarins has a good one. And wear wide sunglasses! If you have very deep crow's feet, use a brightening cream instead of primer and powder. Try Yves Saint Laurent Touche Eclat. (Allure magazine)
