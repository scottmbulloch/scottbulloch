It's time to talk peaches!  
     

All of the peach stands around Fredericksburg are supposed to be open Memorial Day weekend and it is supposed to be a great crop. The different varieties will be ripening throughout June & July. Check the Hill Country Peaches Web site for the stands and ripening dates. Last year there was no peach crop so we have to make up for lost time!

Anti-inflammatory foods  
     

Dr. Andrew Weil has developed a pyramid of foods that help fight inflammation, which is now believed to be a root cause of many age-related diseases. Click here to view the pyramid. Also click on each food to find out more information about amounts and what they do for you.

OK, donuts aren't exactly anti-inflammatory but yum!

Linda and Larry Quinn have been talking about these great donuts in Westlake so I had to try them. I went in to get one and ended up eating three! Light & tasty plus they throw in a few donut holes. Also sausage kolaches and ham & cheese croissants.

Howdy Donuts, M-Sat 6am-1pm; Sun 7am-1pm
3267 Bee Caves Road (corner @ Walsh Tarlton)

I've never been to a paint sale  
     

Sherwin Williams (yes, the paint company) is having a "sunny days" sale… 25% off May 23-31. This may not be your usual summer activity but Dale says these paints are great. They painted the outside of their cabin in New Mexico in 1986 and it still looks good after years of snow, sleet and direct sun. Interior need paint? She says their Relaxed Khaki is a good neutral without being dull. Universal khaki is a darker version.
