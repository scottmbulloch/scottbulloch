It's almost Valentine's Day!  
     

I just love Valentine's Day! Right in the middle of cold, gray days is a holiday about nothing but flowers, love, red & pink.

Restaurants can be crowded and expensive on this day so how about…

St. Valentine's Dinner at Central Market Cooking School with Chef David Bull, Executive Chef, Bolla, Stoneleigh Hotel
This James Beard Award winning Chef returns with a dazzling, romantic menu of:

  • Beef Tartare with Fried Oysters, Asparagus, Crème Fraîche & Caviar
  • Crisp Sea Scallops with Artichoke Ragout, Montasio & Brown Butter
  • Poached Lobster with Citrus Onion Confit, Arugula & Meyer Lemon Butter
  • Chocolate Almond Silk Tart

Thursday, February 4, 6:30-9pm, $70

The Casserole Queens: Celebrate Valentine's Day @ Whole Foods Market
Thursday, February 11, 6:30-8:30pm, $45

WFM welcomes back the Casserole Queens, well known for their delicious home delivery casseroles and invited to compete against Bobby Flay on Throwdown. They'll extend beyond their casserole offerings by demonstrating a delicious romantic meal. Menu: Spring Salad with Gorgonzola-Stuffed Figs Wrapped in Prosciutto; Grilled Ramps with Romesco Sauce; Mashed Celeriac; Pastry Beef Pies with Horseradish Cream; Triple Chocolate Mousse Parfait.


Organic chocolates from a Fair Trade, Green Web site

Taraluna features scarves, home products, food, jewelry and much more from fair trade and eco-friendly sources. For Valentine's Day they have organic chocolates and a European assortment. Margaret Patterson said she has never been disappointed in the items from this company.
