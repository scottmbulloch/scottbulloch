Personal shopper Dale Coker's recs for classic looks  
     

Dale's warnings: Be careful of big print skirts, capri pants, ¾ sleeve jackets…all can make you look dumpy. As Dale says about capris: do NOT wear unless u r thin!--think Mary Tyler Moore--in the fifties--on the DICK Van Dyke show--not us in OUR fifties--!!!-or sixties!

This is also a time for scarves. This Ann Taylor page shows some ways to wear them. Check out TJ Maxx and Marshall's for scarves also and at good prices…but be selective!
