Can't make it to NYC? More fashion's night out in Austin  
     
Last week I mentioned that Saks would be having a fashion's night out with makeovers and cocktails. Neiman Marcus will also participate fashion's night out on September 8, 6-8pm. Go to NeimanMarcus.com/fashionsnightout for more information.

By the way the Saks event is 6-9pm.

    
Tranquillo by Jan Heaton  
     
Well-known artist Jan Heaton will open her latest exhibit at Wally Workman Gallery with a reception September 10. I just love her colors and flowing shapes taken from nature. You may preview the show at WallyWorkman.com. It continues through October 1.

    
Wine dinner at Fonda San Miguel  
     
It had been a long time since I had been to FSM and I went recently with friends, It was as good as I remembered and more. And they are doing a Copain Wine Dinner (along with Austin Wine Merchant) soon. I couldn't decide which dishes to tell you about so I'm just listing them all.
  • Reception
    Salpicon and Guacamole Sopecitos
    Lobster-Mango Ceviche Tostadas
    2009 Tous Ensemble Viognier, Mendocino, California
  • First Course
    Grilled Gulf Snapper
    2009 Tous Ensemble Pinot Noir, Anderson Valley, California
  • Second Course
    Cochinita Pibil with Fried Plantains
    2009 Copain Pinot Noir, Wentzel, Anderson Valley, California
    2009 Copain Pinot Noir, Monument, Anderson Valley, California
  • Third Course
    Grilled Sweetbread Tacos with seasonal sauce
    2009 Tous Ensemble Syrah, Mendocino, California
  • Fourth Course
    Braised Beef Short Ribs with Chipolte Potatoes
    2007 Copain Syrah, Alder Springs, Mendocino, California
    2007 Copain Syrah, Hawks Butte, Yorkville Highlands, California
$85 per person (not inclusive of tax and gratuity). Please contact Fonda San Miguel directly for reservations at (512) 459-4121. Wed., Sept 14 at 6:30 pm
