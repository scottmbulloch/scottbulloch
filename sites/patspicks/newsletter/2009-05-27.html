It's Pops Concert Time! Featuring Cherish the Ladies  
     
I just love packing up a picnic supper and sharing with friends at the Pops Concerts. This time they are featuring the most successful group in Celtic music. Their music and step dancing will be a real treat!

Don't cook? Stuff some cherry tomatoes, feta cheese and cucumber in a pita and add dressing. Maybe pick up some chicken and pork pate at Whole Foods. For a preview of their music go to Amazon. Tickets are $45 and available at The Austin Symphony.
