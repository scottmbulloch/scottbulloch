Tea Time update  
     
Although the Tea Embassy has been in business in its historic home since 2004, Jan Moody recently visited and sampled teas at one of their monthly samplers. She had three courses with four types of teas and goodies from Sandra Bullock's Walton's Bakery and Cafe. Sounds yummy! Jan especially liked the Austin Ambrosia and Almond-coconut cookie teas.

Call (512)330-9991 or check their Web site for monthly dates.

More about green tea  
     
We've heard about the antioxidants, called polyphenols, in green tea for some time, but Dr. Oz says they are primarily in brewed green tea, not the bottled version. I drink decaf and sometimes that's hard to find. I found a good one at Whole Foods made by Allegro.
