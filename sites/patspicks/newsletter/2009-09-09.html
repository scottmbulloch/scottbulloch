Looking Younger… Yes, You can!  
     

Health magazine lists five things that are instant agers:

  1. Heavy foundation- cakes between fine lines.
  2. Clumpy mascara- Curl lashes and, starting at lash base, rock the wand horizontally and roll out to tips.
  3. Side swiped blush- Blush should be on the apples of your cheeks, not high on the cheekbone.
  4. Light-washed jeans- Darker is more flattering. Mid-rise waist and boot-cut.
  5. Anything boxy or tight.

One of my favorite books is How Not to Look Old by Charla Krupp. She gives lots of reminders about keeping your look updated. I noticed that Charla will be in town for Hospice Austin's Beauty of Life Fall Fashion Fundraiser on Tuesday, September 15. $100
