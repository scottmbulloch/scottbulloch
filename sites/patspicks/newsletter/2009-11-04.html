Prepare your face for winter!  
     

With winter just around, if you haven't switched from a lotion moisturizer to a cream, this is the time. To compensate for the dry, cold winds use a cream moisturizer with retinol (for wrinkles!) at night. Health magazine suggests something like RoC Retinol Correction Deep Wrinkle Night Cream. And for the eyes: Clarins eye cream with Vitamin K or L'Oreal Paris Revitalift Anti-Wrinkle & Firming Eye Cream.
