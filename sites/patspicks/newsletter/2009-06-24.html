Summer Reads  
     

Summer and a good book just seem to go together. If the pool or beach are too hot right now, curl up in your favorite chair in the air conditioning. Some time ago I told you about Good Reads, a Web site for book lovers to get book suggestions and recommend books. It's also sort of like Facebook because you can have friends and see their comments. A friend who moved out of state several years ago found me!

Book Clubs
My new neighbor recently moved here from Seattle. Some of her favorite books from her Seattle book club include:

The Lemon Tree by Sandy Tolan - The Palestinian-Israeli conflict as seen through a Palestian refugee and his friend, a Jewish settler.

Interpreter of Maladies by Jhumpa Lahiri - a wonderful collection of short stories about Indian immigrant to the US. (also The Namesake) Pulitzer Prize winner

The Killer Angels by Michael Shaara - One of the best Civil War novels. The story of the Battle of Gettysburg as told by the men who fought there. Pulitzer Prize winner

Lighter Fare (what I'm reading!)
The Ex-Debutante by Linda Francis Lee - A satire about the symphony association debutantes in Willow Creek, Texas. Fast, funny read.

The Third Tower Up from the Road by Kevin Dolgin - Collection of humorous columns written about his unusual travels.


    
Good for You Foods  
     

Dr. Andrew Weil lists the top 10 foods for preventing osteoporosis:

  1. Low-fat or non-fat dairy products
  2. Canned sardines and canned salmon
  3. Dark green vegetables such as broccoli, collard greens and bok choy
  4. Tofu
  5. Calcium-fortified juices and carrageenan free soy milk
  6. Black-eyed peas
  7. Blackstrap molasses
  8. Poppy seeds and sesame seeds
  9. Almonds
  10. Figs

No sign of osteoporosis? Then let's move on to the top six foods for better sex!

  1. Asparagus - Vitamin B6, folate & vitamin E
  2. Avocado
  3. Chile Peppers - The capsaicin releases endorphins
  4. Chocolate - Of course
  5. Licorice - Make sure you get a kind that has real licorice-root extract, not just flavoring
  6. Oysters - It's the zinc, not the texture of the things
