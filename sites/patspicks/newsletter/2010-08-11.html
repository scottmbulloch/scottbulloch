More good news about grapes  
     

From RealAge.com, "The resveratrol in grapes has long been heralded for its heart-healthy properties. But the same compound may also help reduce symptoms of osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis, writes Joseph Maroon, MD, in his book The Longevity Factor."


    
Cast Paper Sculpture  
     

Allen and Patty Eckman of South Dakota put paper pulp into clay molds and pressurize it to remove the water. The hard, lightweight pieces are then removed and detailed finishings are added with a wide range of tools. Some of their creations are life size and others are scaled down to 1/6 size. They sell for up to $130,000. Thanks Elaine for the article!
