Stuck in 100+ degree heat?  
     
I used to think that visiting my son in Phoenix during the summer was a crazy idea but Austin temps have been higher than Phoenix! Time to search for options.
Kayak.com/explore offers a tool to let you search for flights, hotels, etc in areas worldwide. Enter your departure city and specify a time frame and the site will give you the options and cost. You may enter filters for price, region, and flight duration. It gives you an idea what travel to different areas will cost.

    
Drugstore deals

There are too many products to list them all but you can see them at the Allure Web site. They include makeup, skin care, and hair products.
