National Museum Day  
     

Well, I missed Austin Museum Day last weekend but Saturday, Sept 26 is National Museum Day. Smithsonian Media offers a free for two to any participating museum nationwide. Check for museums and tickets at the Web site. In Austin, it includes the Blanton Museum, the Neill-Cochran House, and the Lady Bird Johnson Wildflower Center. Houston and Dallas have lots of participating museums listed…check for the entire list.
