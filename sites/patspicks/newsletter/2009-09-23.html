Chocolate news! Roscar  
     

Nicole Albers told me about this gourmet chocolate shop in Bastrop. These are no ordinary chocolates: handcrafted of the freshest ingredients. These are artisan chocolates boxed in beautiful domed treasure boxes. One of the top food orders in Texas according to Texas Monthly.

4501 Hwy 71 East, Bastrop, Texas
Tasting Room hours
Wednesday thru Saturday from 10am-6pm
Sunday from Noon-6pm
Closed Monday and Tuesday.

Also available at Breed & Co.
