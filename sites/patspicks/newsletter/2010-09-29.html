Turner to Monet  
     

I've been waiting for this one! The Blanton Museum of Art will host Masterpieces from the Walters Museum, a selection of forty nineteenth-century paintings includes works from Impressionist artists Claude Monet, Camille Pissarro, Édouard Manet and Edgar Degas, as well as British and American masters J.M.W. Turner, Gilbert Stuart and Asher B. Durand. What fabulous examples of 19th century painting!

You'd almost think you were Paris  
     

On my way to the Galleria Mall I stopped by a French bakery I have been wanting to try...Baguette et Chocolat. Divine croissants! And light macarons (the French pastry, not the heavy macaroon) in lots of flavors. Do not let bland, strip center exterior fool you! The owner is originally from Versailles and worked there perfecting his art.

12101 Bee Caves Rd (2244) On the left if you're going west, almost to 71

Small changes, big results  
     

Realage.com has two small changes you can make to start losing weight.

  1. Walk an extra 500 steps a day
  2. Trim an extra 100 calories from one meal each day (that's one less pat of butter or three fewer sips of soda!)

They even have a video about walking indoors if it's too hot, too cold, or too dark outside (isn't it always!)
