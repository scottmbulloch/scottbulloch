It's Pumpkin Patch time!  
     
Tarrytown United Methodist Church Youth (2601 Exposition Blvd) holds a Pumpkin Patch fundraiser each year to help raise money for youth mission and ministry trips throughout the year. The Pumpkin Patch will be open from October 4 through Halloween. The hours of operation are:
Monday - Friday: 2:00 pm to 7:30 pm
Saturday - 9:00 am to 7:30 pm, and
Sunday - 12:00 pm to 7:30 pm.

Goblins in the Garden at the Wildflower Center
Trail of Bones scavenger hunt, pumpkin carving demonstrations, and a Scarecrow Contest! Dress up and bring your camera!

Sprouts
2505 Bee Caves Rd (The other locations may do this also)
Last year they had the biggest assortment of pumpkins out front, complete with a scarecrow!

Richardson Farms tour
The farm and Slow Food Austin will host a tour on Sunday, October 24, at their farm in Rockdale (directions on their Web site). They raise pastured turkey, cattle, chickens, and hogs. Tickets are $20, which goes toward the purchase of farm products. I've tried the steaks, stew meat, ground beef, chicken, and pork chops.. all were good! You can find their products at the Barton Creek Square Farmer's Market also.
