Happy 4th of July!  
     

Time for hot dogs, deviled eggs, baked beans, watermelon, and a flag cake!

Food Network had this interesting take on the flag cake…little tarts with fruit arranged like a flag. I haven't tried it but I think I could do this!

Fill premade miniature tart shells or phyllo cups with sweet mascarpone cream (recipe below), then arrange on a tray and top with blueberries and halved strawberry slices to create stars and stripes.

Mascarpone Cream:
Beat an 8-ounce container of mascarpone cheese and 3 tablespoons of confectioners' sugar with a mixer until smooth. Gently fold in 1/2 cup freshly whipped cream.
