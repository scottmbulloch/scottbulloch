East Austin Urban Farm Tour  
     
Chefs from some of Austin's finest restaurants will prepare dishes at each of the farms. Eric Polzer of WINK and Stewart Scruggs of ZOOT, for example, will be at Boggy Creek Farm. Participating East Austin Urban Farms: Hausbar, Rain Lily, Springdale, Boggy Creek Farm. Simultaneous tours at ALL farms start at 1:15, 2:15, 3:15, 4:15.
April 17, $35 Tickets available online or at the gate
Info also at boggycreekfarm.com (scroll down to tour description)

Easter brunches for those who are challenged
in the culinary arts...		  
     
If you're looking for a traditional Easter brunch you can't do much better than the extensive menu at the Driskill Hotel. Check out the menu. Prime rib, salmon, ham,leg of lamb...there's something for everyone here and set in the historic Driskill. $65 adults, children 6-12 years $32.50, children under 6 complimentary.

For table service at brunch and dinner, Mirabelle has a fabulous menu $31.95, 11am - 3pm and 5-7pm. Asian Lacquered Salmon, Rosemary Crusted Prime Rib...you've just got to read the menu.

Itzhak Perlman in Austin!  
     
The concert isn't until April 28 but I am mentioning it now because I think this will be a hot ticket item. And the concert ends with fireworks on the terrace! Check the Web site for tickets ($35-$150) and program.
