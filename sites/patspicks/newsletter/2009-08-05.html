It's sort of quiet right now. If you have kids going back to school you're probably thinking about what needs to happen before school starts or maybe trying to get out of town for one last weekend.

This gave me a chance to go back and let you know about some things I just didn't have room for before. Here goes!


    
How much is your 401(k) costing you?  
     

Good for you if you work for an employer who sponsors a retirement plan such as a 401(k). But there is a trend toward shifting expenses to the employees (no surprise). Get Rich Slowly is a Web site that provides lots of tips for living well on less, and one of their recent posts reports that there are increasingly more hidden fees for 401(k) accounts. Check the expense ratio of your mutual funds; it should not be more than 1 percent. Evaluate your investment choices; some plans get payments from mutual funds to be included in the plan. For the rest of the article, go to the Website and scroll down to the July 29 post. By the way, you can sign up to get daily emails of the posts.


    
Speaking of blogs…  
     

If you're a foodie, you might checkout Bitten by Chef Mark Bittman or Well, the health and wellness blog by the NY Times.

Or for fashion updates I like Omiru. Everything from figure flattering tips to runway revues. Some of the looks might be a little trendy, but you can use their tips to update any look.

A better way to store jewelry? Fashion magazines use jeweler's velvet trays. These travel displays are $2-$6 and are stackable. Go to travelautobag.com and click on jewelry sample cases.


    
Meteor show  
     

Dale Coker always has the most interesting items: Earth is entering a stream of debris from Comet Swift-Tuttle, setting the stage for the 2009 Perseid meteor shower. On August 11 and 12, we will enter the thickest part of the stream and you should be able to see dozens of meteors each hour. Check the NASA Web site for details.


    
From the Brits with Love  
     

I started noticing Boden in fashion magazines a few years ago and started ordering from them. Love their colors. I wait till things go on sale. They used to have customer reviews but I haven't seen them lately. Also, be careful…these are UK sizes; check the chart for US equivalents.
