And speaking of food, Marilyn Scher has posted her new classes  
     
I'm thinking about that Crunchy Cornmeal & Tomato Galette and Lime-Rum Ice Cream w/Capuccino Coins. Her classes are held in her home in Westlake... informal and fun!

Cooking Classes by Marilyn Scher
Classes for May-June 2009

www.marilynschercooking.com
marilynscher@austin.rr.com
  1. Pickled Cucumber Bites
    Pad Thai w/Spicy Tofu & Shrimp
    Spicy Curry Noodle Soup w/Sweet Potato & Chicken
    Currant-Ginger Shortbreads

    $65/person...
    Wednesday, May 6, 10am
    Tuesday, June 2, 10am

    A wonderful seasoned, but NOT too spicy Asian-inspired menu, every dish of which you'll want to make again and again! The pickled cucumbers are the perfect accompaniment to everything from the most elaborate buffet to a simply great burger; the Pad Thai goes together in a flash and will yield a ton of food for a minimum of effort; the curry noodle soup bursts with color and delicious flavors; the lovely shortbread cookie ends the meal on a simple, sweet note.

  2. Roasted Vegetable and Tahini Dip w/Pita Crisps
    Honey-Lemon Roast Salmon Fillet w/Lemon Cream over
    Fresh Greens
    Roasted Root Vegetable Medley
    Fresh Apricot Tart

    $70/person...
    Thursday, May 14, 10am or 6pm
    Thursday, June 4, 10am or 6pm

    A colorful menu of fresh flavors, heavy on the produce to emphasize the freshness of the season! The vegetable dip is rich and creamy smooth, but contains only vegetables and bit of tahini to bind it; a beautiful salmon entree that is simply divine; easy-as-pie roasted vegetables to round out the plate; a lovely rustic tart with fresh fruit encased in the flakiest pastry imaginable!

  3. Best Crab Cake atop Salad of Frisee, Grapefruit, Avocado & Corn w/Champagne Vinaigrette
    Crunchy Cornmeal & Tomato Galette
    Open-Faced Fig Sandwiches w/Arugula & Shaved Parmesan
    Lime-Rum Ice Cream w/Capuccino Coins

    $70/person...
    Wednesday, May 20, 10am or 6pm
    Monday, June 8, 10am or 6pm

    The lightest, most flavorful crab cakes are the star of this gorgeous entree salad; the galette can serve as a simple entree or a luxurious appetizer; ditto for the fig sandwiches; a fabulous no-egg recipe for velvety smooth ice cream accompanied by delectable tiny mocha-flavored cookies... betcha can't eat just one!
