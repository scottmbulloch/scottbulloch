Sousa on the Square  
     
Sounds like a fun, old fashioned Sunday afternoon in the park. The Austin Symphony Brass Quintet will play John Philip Sousa in Wooldridge Park to celebrate the opening of an exhibit at the Austin History Center about the history of the Austin Symphony.

Sunday, October 2, concert at 2:30pm. The exhibit will be open 12-6pm.

AIA Homes Tour  
     
Fifteen architects will be showing their designs as part of the homes tour Oct 1 & 2, 10am - 6pm. Everything from renovations to quaint homes to contemporary new construction. Visit the website for photos of the homes, a map, purchasing tickets ($25-$30).
