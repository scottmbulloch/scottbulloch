Wear Art Thou?  
     
When Boatright Floors moved from Marathon to their new location on Burnet Road, Karen Boatright made a great discovery! The business moving into their old location was a unique boutique that carries casual women's clothing in natural fibers, one-of-a-kind jewelry made by the owner, beautiful scarves and much more! Even better, they carry everything from petites to 3X.

It's not surprising the clothing and jewelry are artistic and unique, the owner is a designer by training and has designed for the Miss America pageant. They don't advertise; it's word of mouth only and now you know!
Wear Art Thou
Tues-Sat 10-6
4107 Marathon

    
Beethoven and Wine and Duchman Wine  
     
Bring your Valentine for an evening of music and wine at Duchman Family Winery. The evening will feature music by Beethoven and Mendelssohn, performed by the newly formed Austin Pro Musica Chamber Orchestra. You will enjoy a hand chosen wine tasting, lovely music and the company of your loved one, all in a romantic setting.

Tickets per couple are only $65 at the door, cash or check only please. To make a reservation, please call Melissa at 512.858.1470 ext. 107
