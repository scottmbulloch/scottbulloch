Ready to ditch the disposable water bottles?

If you're ready to switch to reusable water bottles, there are lots of choices out there. Plastic, Stainless Steel, Glass... Plastic is the lightest but there is quite a bit of controversy about the chemicals in plastics, especially polycarbonate, which is thought to leach Bisphenol A (BPA). Try the no. 2 plastic (HDPE) rather than the no. 7. The Container Store has a 16 oz. no. 2 bottle for $3.49. Cheaper and safer (though not as pretty) than the Lexan polycarbonate models. If you have a favorite reusable water bottle let us know (info@patspicksaustin.com) and we'll post other suggestions.
