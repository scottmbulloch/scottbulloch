Tips for safer online shopping  
     
Online shopping can save time & energy but, especially during this busy time, protect yourself from scams.
  1. Save your credit card information in fewer places. It is better not to save your credit card info on the Web site. Take the time to re-enter it each time.
  2. Avoid online shopping in public. Even if you're using your own phone, your info is at risk.
  3. Use a credit card, not a debit card. Credit cards usually have better protections against theft.
  4. Make sure the Web site you are using has an "https" at the beginning of their address or a lock icon, usually beside or under their Web address.
  5. Avoid going to a Web site using banner ads. Type in the specific Web address.
