Kelly Bolinger received a diagnosis of brain cancer shortly after having her first baby. A recipe for women who don't cook!  
     

Myra Schmidt makes this delicious soup sans measurements. This is my best approximation. It can be pulled together REALLY fast but tastes like you've been cooking all afternoon.

Italian soup

  • 1 lb mild Italian sausage
  • ½ onion
  • 1 large can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can Rotel (less if you don't want much spice)
  • Chicken bouillon (I used almost a box)
  • Few cloves garlic or garlic powder
  • Bunch of spinach
  • Brown meat and onion.

Add everything else and heat thoroughly.
