Ridge Vineyards Portfolio Tasting at Mirabelle  
     

It may be too late for this tasting, which is tonight (Wed, April 14, at 6:30), but call 346-7900 to check. Ridge winemaker John Olney will do a portfolio tasting of Ridge's new releases.

There will be a number of wine dinners at Mirabelle in May, which we will keep you posted about.


    
Tuesdays mean Jazz!  
     

Mirabelle hosts "The Occasionals" almost every Tuesday night at 7pm. Call 346-7900 for reservations. No additional charge.

Vino Vino on Guadalupe has Blues artist Gary Clark, Jr, on Mondays at 9pm when he's in town and the smooth jazz sounds of Kat Edmonson on Tuesday at 7pm when she's in town. Best to call to see who's playing. And this is one of the best places to enjoy a glass of wine on any occasion.

Foods that are poisonous to pets  
     

The top pet poisoning claims in 2008, according to pet health insurance company VPI, were for raisins and grapes. The following foods may be poisonous to your pets:

avocados, beer, chocolate, candy, caffeine, grapes and raisins, nuts, onions, some human medicines, xylitol (sweetner), lillies.

Are you a child of the 60s?  
     

The Art of Grace Slick

Art on 5th Gallery, Friday, April 16, 6:30-9

Grace Slick, lead singer of the 60's rock group Jefferson Airplane, is an accomplished artist and will be showing a wide range of paintings, including portraits of some of her rock contemporaries. Two Q&A sessions by closed circuit will be held during the evening. Takes me back!


    
It's Fiesta time in San Antonio!  
     

Folks in San Antonio know what this means: 10 days jammed with over 100 events. There are so many you really need to check their Web site.

Some of the highlights are the four nights of NIOSA (Night in Old San Antonio), the River Parade on April 19 at 7pm, the Battle of Flowers Parade on April 23 at 11:50pm, and the Fiesta Flambeau Parade at 7pm
