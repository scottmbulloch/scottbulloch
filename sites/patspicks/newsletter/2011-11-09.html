The Magic of Christmas  
     
Let the shopping begin! Christmas Affair runs Nov 17-20 with lots of vendors and special events. $12, check Web Site for prices for special events and parking.

    
Thanksgiving Dinner the easy way  
     
If you don't have family in town or just don't feel like cooking here are some great choices for dinner out. Click on the restaurant name for the full menu. Reservations are a must at most!

Trio at the Four Seasons
Trio always delivers with an elegant buffet. $75

III Forks
Featuring turkey or Châteaubriand and all the trimmings. $42.95

Kerby Lane Cafe
Down home cooking for a friendly dinner. It will be crowded but good!

Threadgill's
Comfort food in an Austin tradition. Ditto for crowds!

Lake Austin Spa & Resort
And for a real treat, sign up for a half day or full day package and enjoy Thanksgiving dinner at the spa.

    
The Quest for macarons  
     
My friend Nicole Albers brought macarons back from Paris but I don't think I can count on that a permanent source. I found a tiny French bakery in South Austin that has macarons, eclairs, other things yummy! It only has two cases of goodies but the things I tried were great. They also serve sandwiches for lunch. La Patisserie at 602 W. Annie (off S. First)
