The good eating doesn't end on Thanksgiving day  
     
From cooking instructor Marilyn Scher:
Favorite Turkey Soup
  • 1 T. vegetable or olive oil
  • 3 lg. ribs celery, cut in small dice
  • 2 lg. carrots, peeled and cut in small dice
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 4 c. turkey or chicken broth
  • 2 cups diced leftover turkey meat
  • 1 can cream-style corn
  • ¼ c. chopped fresh parsley leaves
  • ¾ tsp. kosher salt
  • Several dashes Tabasco sauce
  • 1 cup small bow-tie pasta (uncooked)
In large soup pot, heat oil and saute onion, celery and carrot over medium heat until beginning to soften, stirring occasionally. Add the broth, canned corn, diced turkey, salt, pepper and Tabasco. Bring to a slow boil, add the pasta, cover pot, lower heat and cook for about 12-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until pasta is done. Add the parsley, and cook another minute or two.
Serves 4-6.
Marilyn Scher, 2011
