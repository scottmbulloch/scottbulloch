Harvest Wine Trail August 14-16 and 21-23  
     

It's still a little over two weeks away but tickets to wine trails sell out. On this trail you will get a book to collect labels and take at each winery. Twenty-four wineries will be offering special activities and specials (a three-bottle selection discounted 15 percent). For details and to buy tickets go to their Web site.


    
Not your ordinary tacos!  
     

Jody Richardson has another tasty restaurant tip for us… Garrido's. David Garrido, former executive chef of Jeffrey's has opened his own restaurant featuring tacos. But not just any tacos. How about coffee marinated ribeye steak with queso asadero and chipotle horseradish aioli? Or grilled Mahi Mahi BLT with habanero aioli. Yum! You can order them with rice and beans or a la carte. Jody suggests getting different kinds and splitting them so everyone gets to try more than one kind!
360 Nueces & 3rd St, Suite 10


    
Emerging artists at reasonable prices  
     

Have great taste but not the pocketbook to match? 20 x 200 is a Web site that offers prints of emerging artists at $20 for an 8 x 10, $50 for an 11 x 14, and $200 for a 16 x 20. They present two new pieces each week: one photo and one work on paper. Lots of styles.
