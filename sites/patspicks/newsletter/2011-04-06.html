A new retreat location for women who love
to sew and quilt		  
     
Creations, the sewing enthusiasts' source for fabrics and supplies in Kerrville has expanded to include a guest house for groups who want to hold a retreat in the Hill Country. Check out the Main House

Lou Ann Caywood got a preview tour and loves the place! Continental breakfast is provided and there is an electric griddle, but there is also a list of caterers if you're not in the mood to cook. The Main house be open April 7-10 for visitors to check it out.

    
Make a fist to make better food decisions  
     
Weird but there is scientific evidence that it may help boost your willpower to make a fist when tempted by yummy, calorie-rich foods. In a recent study reported by RealAge.com, squeezing muscles at the moment of decision seemed to help prompt people to make healthier snack choices.

Antioxidants may help prevent damage from CT scans  
     
As someone who has had enough CT scans to light the city of Dallas, I was interested to see this note from Alegria Hibbetts. At the Society of Interventional Radiology's 36th Annual Scientific Meeting held in Chicago this month, Kieran J. Murphy, MD, FSIR reports that a combination of antioxidants consumed prior to medical imaging studies involving ionizing radiation could help protect against their damaging effects. They used vitamin C and glutathione.
