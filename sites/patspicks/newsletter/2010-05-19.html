How long have you had that mascara?  
     

All it takes are a few cells from an unwashed hand or a blistered lip and a mascara wand or lip gloss could become a haven for contagious bacteria. Good Morning America gave these tips for preventing contamination:

  1. Sharpen your eye and lip pencils to remove the bacteria on the outer layers. Also use diluted bleach and makeup remover to clean your sharpener.
  2. Wipe down your lipstick, or shave off the top of it with a blade especially if you have shared it with someone else.
  3. Keep the lids and caps tightly closed. If you lost them, throw it out.
  4. Use disposable applicators or wash your brushes every few weeks.

Shelf life of makeup:

  • Mascara: Three months
  • Concealer: 12-18 months
  • Cream Blush: 12-18 months
  • Cream Eye shadow: 12-18 months
  • Lip gloss: 18-24 months
  • Powder: Two years
