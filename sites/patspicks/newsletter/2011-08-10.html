For those days when you need a really yummy dessert FAST!  
     
This recipe from Sandy Perkins is perfect. (It, or something very similar, is also served at the Austin Woman's Club)

Lemon-Lime Surprise
  • 1 6oz can frozen (keep it frozen) lemonade or limeade
  • 1 16oz cool whip
  • 1 can Eagle Brand condensed milk
Mix all together
Option 1- pour into 2 graham cracker crusts and chill
Option 2- pour into sherbet glasses and chill

Topping suggestions:
Dollop of whipped cream & toasted cocunut
Fresh strawberries or raspberries
Fill cups made from phyllo cups
Similar to key lime pie, only fast
