Weird hint for the kitchen  
     
Ever pulled foil out and the whole roll came out of the box? Am I the only one who never noticed the little tab at the end of the box that says, 'Press here to lock end'?

Right there on the end of the box is a tab to lock the roll in place. How long has this little locking tab been there? It's on the box of Saran wrap too. Can't believe I never noticed that thing!
