Non-organic foods to eat  
     

We've talked about which fruits and vegetables you should choose organic to eat but here's a list from Dr. Andrew Weil of the foods that are the least likely to be contaminated. Some of these are surprising to me. Good place to save $$$.

Onions, Avocados, Sweet corn, Pineapples, Mangos, Asparagus, Sweet peas, Kiwi, Cabbage, Eggplant, Papaya, Watermelon, Broccoli, Tomatoes, Sweet potatoes
