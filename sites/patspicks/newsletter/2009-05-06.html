Happy Mother's Day  
     
Still trying to think of something special to do on Mother's Day this weekend (May 10)? If it's too late to get reservations at your favorite restaurant, how about a drive through the Hill Country to a Central Texas winery for lunch?

Fall Creek Vineyards, Driftwood Vineyards, and Becker Vineyards are offering fabulous meals paired with their wines. Try Chicken with Scallop Mousse Roulade with Pesto Cream (Becker) or Sauteed Fresh Water White Basa Fish with Raphael Sauce (Fall Creek). For more information check listings under the "events" tab at the top of this page.

If Mom loves pottery or just loves beautiful handcrafted work, take her to the Art of the Pot, at Westlake High School for the first time this year. Sixteen Central Texas artists and out-of-state invited artists not usually seen in Austin will show and sell unique items. Live music and demonstrations. May 9 (10 - 6) & 10 (12 - 5) at Westlake HS.

    
And for a real treat:  
     
Give Mom or yourself a facial or massage at Frou Frou, an exclusive salon at 816 W. 12th St (469-7800). Fun, pink interior and great service PLUS during May get $20 off any facial and $30 off any massage. They also carry top of the line products such as SkinCeuticals and Kinerase.

Just FYI  
     
We knew that red wine was good for you but for a bath? Health magazine reports that the Four Seasons Resort The Biltmore Santa Barbara uses this soak for healthier skin:
  • 20 drops jasmine essential oil
  • ¼ c grape seed oil
  • 1 c Epsom or sea salt
  • ½ bottle red wine
Soak for 20 minutes. I guess you drink the other ½ bottle of the red wine!
