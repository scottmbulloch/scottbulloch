Quick eats  
     
I am always looking for quick, tasty dishes that can be prepared ahead of time. This dish from Patti Hirsh fits all of these requirements. Patti says it comes from the yellow Junior League cookbook. I loved it!

1 can yellow corn
1 can french style green beans
1 can peas
Drain all liquid and pour vegetables into a bowl
Add chopped celery, green onion or red onion, red bell pepper

Dressing-
1/3 cup oil
1/3 cup white vinegar
1/3 cup sugar

Mix all together and refrigerate for 2-3 hours
Enjoy!
