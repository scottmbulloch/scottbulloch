Appeals from readers:  
     

from Jan Moody:

Pioneer Farms is racing against the clock to raise the final $8,000 needed to save the historic Jarmon House from a scheduled demolition at the end of January. Built around 1900, this one-story house is one of the most important historical landmarks in northeastern Travis County. The landfill adjacent to the property is expanding and intends to demolish this irreplaceable treasure. With no other alternative to save it from that fate, Pioneer Farms has committed to raising the money necessary to move the Jarmon House to the Farms. To contribute, click here.

More about almonds…  
     

We all know that almonds are good for you but do you know why? Dr. Andrew Weil says they have:

  1. Monounsaturated fats that help reduce the risk of heart disease
  2. Protein to provide sustained energy
  3. Calcium for strong and healthy bones
  4. Magnesium, which promotes normal blood pressure and healthy metabolism

Eat unsalted or low salt almonds that are raw or dry roasted.
