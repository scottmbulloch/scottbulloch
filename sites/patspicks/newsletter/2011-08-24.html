Missoni-Target lines  
     
Did you see that the latest Designer partner for target is Missoni? The characteristic Missoni zigzags will appear on clothing, housewares, shoes, and more. I am especially interested in seeing the home items. The collection will arrive September 13 and will be available until October 22 (or as long as supplies last). View the lookbook.


    
Designer mascara?  
     
While you're at Target check out the special packing by designers Tracy Reese, Vivienne Tam , and Max Azria for Maybelline's iconic pink mascara tube. It's a limited edition to celebrate 40 years of the Great Lash mascara.

    
Joshua Bell returns to the Austin Symphony  
     
The Austin Symphony will open it's season on September 8 and 9 with violinist Joshua Bell. There's a reason he has been invited back repeatedly. The Boston Globe has called Mr. Bell "the greatest American violist active today." More information and to view the entire season click here.
