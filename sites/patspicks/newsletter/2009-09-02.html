It's September and you know what that means…  
     

…the big September issues of the fashion magazines! As a public service here is a Reader's Digest version of what's new. But keep your style and needs in mind. You just may not be the thigh-high boot type.

Boyfriend jackets - oversized. Or add shoulder pads to any jacket. Personally, I just can't go back to the 80s, but that what's being shown.

Burnished metallics - Metallics have been around but they are softer.

Leather jackets - especially bomber jackets

Animal prints - You may only need a touch of animal to look updated; you don't need to be the entire leopard.

Suits - 40s looks with peplums or highly textured

Cuffs - Bold, wide

Chain necklaces - Larger & bolder; shades of gray, pewter, gunmetal
Also statement (big) necklaces that may have abstract designs to florals.

Large clutches - A clutch, but big enough to really hold stuff

Boots - short, ankle-high or really tall over the knee

One shoulder dresses are still good
