March 31, 2010
New Works by Jan Heaton  
     

Austin artist Jan Heaton has been in Southern California for several months, and the simplistic beauty and healing properties of nature have inspired her latest watercolor collection, Laguna. This is a very personal collection that celebrates life and natural beauty. Wally Workman Gallery will show the collection April 3-28 with an opening reception April 3, 6-8 pm.


    
Faculty Artist Concert  
     

If you missed Anton Nel playing with the Austin Symphony Orchestra a little over a year, he will be playing at the Butler School of Music at the UT Bates Recital Hall at 7:30 pm on April 3. They will present works by Robert Schumann and Frederic Chopin and Samuel Barber. $10


    
Speaking of Artists…  
     

If you plan to be in New York between April 27 and August 1, stop by the Metropolitan Museum of Art. A landmark exhibit of 250 of Pablo Picasso's works will feature the museum's entire collection, which has not been seen in its entirety before.


    
Lang Lang…in Austin!  
     

Called the "hottest artist on the classical music planet" by The New York Times, Lang Lang is perhaps the greatest classical pianist of the day and will be backed by Christoph Eschenbach. One night, April 13 at 8 pm at the Long Center.


    
If you never miss the Texas Hill Country Wine and Food Festival, you need to hurry!  
     

So many of their events sell out and the event is April 15-18. Check the Web site for times and locations of all the events. Wonderful winemaker luncheons but they sell out!


    
The Vineyard at Florence  
     

Marilyn Scher, cooking class instructor and columnist, will be teaching a class about entree salads at the Vineyard on Thursday, April 15. $75

And that weekend the Vineyard will host the Boutique Wine and Food Festival in association with Inwood Estates Vineyards and co-sponsored by Ambiente and Edible Austin (Saturday, April 17 from 11:00 am to 6:00 pm) with 13 wineries. $35

I've never been to the development but they have lots of beautiful amenities for wine lovers and equestrians…sounds like a road trip!


    
Last Call specials  
     

Joyce Moeller says that Last Call now sends out email to announce 30 percent discounts on certain items on certain days. On March 23, for example, women's sportswear and dresses were 30 percent off. If you subscribe to the emails you also get coupons.

More good news about Bananas!  
     

We knew they had lots of potassium but Drs. Roizen and Oz at Realage.com report that bananas also have phenolic compounds that appear to prevent renal-cancer cells. Plus they're a good source of B vitamins.


    
Note to grandmothers  
     

If you're looking for cute baby and toddler clothes without spending a fortune, check out Target and JC Penny. Several grandmothers have shown me things they found there and they are adorable. But buy them when you see them because they sell quickly!
