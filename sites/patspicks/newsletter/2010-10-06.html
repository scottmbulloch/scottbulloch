Hurrah! Good news about cheese!  
     

I love cheese (fat and all) and now Realage.com reports that a German study indicates that cheese may be a cancer fighter. cheese is a rich source of menaquinones, a type of vitamin K that switches on genes in the body that knock out cancer cells. But don't rely on cheese alone, because you'll get an overload of artery-clogging, calorie-laden saturated fat, too. Egg yolks and chicken are also good sources...and leafy greens.
