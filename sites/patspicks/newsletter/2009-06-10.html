Looking for something local?  
     
The Blanco Lavender Festival is June 12-14. Tour the local lavender farms free or visit the market in downtown Blanco. Just what you need to sooth you... a selection of lavender products! And in the park there's Zydeco, Texas' blues, and gospel music. Check the website for times for specific events and driving directions.

Renee Trudeau is offering a FREE sampler teleclass on June 11 to see what personal self renewal is all about. Maybe you need more balance in your life. Register here.

Tomato Time! Get back to nature and pick your veggies at Boggy Creek Farm. Hours are Wednesday & Saturday, 9-1 but better get there at least by 11am. Tomatoes, potatoes, & zinnias are best now. And June 20 is the Annual Tomato Festival with down-home music!
