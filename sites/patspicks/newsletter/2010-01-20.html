Confused by the new credit card laws?  
     

Next month the second round of credit card laws will go into effect. Some of you may have already noticed as card companies rushed to change policies before the new laws take effect.

Since the changes are rather long but significant, I hate to summarize too much. Here is the best summary I've seen… on Get Rich Slowly

For example, credit card rates can't be increased on outstanding balances — except for when a 0% or other low interest introductory rate expires on newly-issued cards or when a customer is 60 days late on a payment.
