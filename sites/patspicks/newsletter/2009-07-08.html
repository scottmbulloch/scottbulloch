A journey right at home  
     

Take an inward journey this summer with Nancy Oelklaus. Nancy likes to see people become happier and more peaceful. They come to become better leaders or to hold onto a job or to save a marriage. Although there are several programs, there is a unique one called the Fourth Friday Group Lunch. No more than four participants gather at the beautiful Green Pastures Restaurant from 11 to 2 to address one specific issue that each is dealing with and get tools to help them resolve the issue. $85, includes lunch. You may check all the programs at the Web site.


    
A tisket a tasket  
     

I've had trouble finding a straw bag this summer (can you believe it?) but I found these Moroccan baskets that are imported by a family-run business in the Blue Ridge Mountains of Virginia. Medina Baskets works through the Global Diversity Foundation to promote small farms. I haven't gotten one yet but they look well-made and almost all are under $50.


    
More sustainable products  
     

Spirit Beauty Lounge has primarily bath and body products (lots of organics), but they also have bags and jewelry. And what I really like is that for $10-$15 you may order a sample selection of their products to try before you buy.

Speaking of organics, my daughter-in-law Kim swears by Pangea Organics. Made in small batches of pure organics and encourages sustainable farming. Available at many yoga studios and Cost Plus Market.
