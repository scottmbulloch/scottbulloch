Give Spinach some light  
     

Research reported by RealAge.com shows that bags of spinach that were under fluorescent light increased the amount of nutrients while bags that were stored in darkness stayed the same or decreased. They think that the light may help the photosynthesis process continue. Thanks Alegria!
