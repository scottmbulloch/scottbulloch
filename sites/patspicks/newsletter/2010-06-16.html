Comfort Brands  
     

The New York Times recently ran an article about clothing brands that are going through their archives to bring back styles that were popular anywhere from 1910 to 1957. Consumers just aren't spending and the belief is that these heritage lines will not only meet the feel-good factor but will meet consumers' demands for real value.

LL Bean, Keds, Lands' End, Eddie Bauer are among the heritage lines. Even Cheerios and Pepsi will have retro packaging. And sure enough, I picked up a copy of Bazaar and there was an ad for Jantzen that could have been in a 1955 magazine. And after all the skin-exposing styles I've been seeing, I've got to admit the one-piece suit on a woman with her hair combed was sort of nice. No pouting and snarling at the camera here! (Do I sound old or what?)
