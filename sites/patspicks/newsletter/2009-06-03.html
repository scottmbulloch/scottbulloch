3 Must-Have Vitamins for your Skin  
     
Drs. Oz and Roizen (the "YOU" book authors) say you need vitamin A, especially in the form of retinoids (or the milder retinol in many creams)
Vitamin C - promotes the production of collagen. Look for products that contain at least a 10 percent concentration of L-ascorbic acid
Vitamin E - An antioxidant that prevents wrinkles. Look for dl-alpha-tocopherol on labels.
