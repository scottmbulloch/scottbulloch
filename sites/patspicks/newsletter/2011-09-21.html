Salvador Dali — The Argillet Collection  
     
A collection of drawings, etchings, tapestries and watercolors from art collector and publisher Pierre Argillet who was a friend of Dali's and watched as Dali developed the techniques that made him a renowned Surrealist. Pierre's daughter Christine will give a presentation.

VIP reception, Sept. 23, 6-9 pm
Opening reception, Sept 24, 6-9 pm
RSVP please to 478-4440
