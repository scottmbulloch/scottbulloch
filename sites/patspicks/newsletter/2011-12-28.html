New Year's Eve  
     
If you want to do something special on New Year's Eve but you're not into the parties, how about a couple of relaxing nights at a lovely hotel. You might try the Mokara in San Antonio, the only four-star hotel in the city. On the Riverwalk. It's probably too late to get in on the packages but it would be a great getaway.

    
Spa time!  
     
Or use this time for a day at the spa. Artist Jan Heaton's daughter Kristin has opened Hiatus Spa at 1611 W. 5th St. (across from El Arroyo). Opening specials include essential services for $49 (two for $89). Call for details. 362-5777

    
Wishing you a happy, healthy, loving new year!
