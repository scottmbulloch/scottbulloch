What is this vegetable and what do I do with it?  
     
With all the wonderful produce in the farmer's markets now, sometimes you find things that you may not be sure how to select and prepare. It's time to be adventurous!

How to Pick a Peach by Russ Parsons tells you the development of our fruits & vegetables, how to select them and then store & prepare them (& recipes). We found a copy at Book People. Want to keep green beans a vibrant green? Cook in lots of water, return water to boil quickly, cook fast & plunge in cold water. The Produce Bible by Leanne Kitchen also looked promising.

And Michael Chiarello (of Food Network fame) has a blog that discusses fresh produce, including the grapes he grows for wine.
http://michaelchiarello.com/blog/
