A Facelift in 5 Minutes  
     
From Health magazine, six steps to look years younger with make-up.
  1. Use sheer, hydrating, light-reflective foundation. No opaque foundation.
  2. Fill in brows with a shade lighter than your hair color. A full brow with a low arch is most youthful.
  3. Conceal and curl. Use a creamy concealer under eyes, two shades lighter than your skin tone. Curl lashes.
  4. Brush shadow up to meet brows at the outer corners for "an instant wake up call," according to Maria Verel, make up artist for Good Morning America.
  5. "Peachy, pink, and coral-toned blushes are the ultimate skin-brighteners," says Barbie Laurino, makeup artist for Laura Mercier.
  6. Lighten your lips with a subtle color and sheer, understated pink gloss with gold flecks... just the right amount of color. No lip liner. Bobbi Brown suggests one shade brighter than your natural lip color since lips get paler.
