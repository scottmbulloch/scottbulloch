This flower may lower your blood pressure  
     

Drinking 3 cups of hibiscus tea per day appears to help lower systolic blood pressure by an average of seven points in people with prehypertension or mildly elevated blood pressure according to the YOU doctors. Also use less salt in cooking, exercise daily, and reduce stress. Relaxation exercise video.
