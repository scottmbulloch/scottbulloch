ARC Dermatology and Med Spa opens!

The Austin Regional Clinic has opened eOs, a med spa offering Botox, dermal fillers, hair removal, microdermabrasion and spa services such as lash & brow tinting, hand & foot hydration and even airbrush tanning. And all under the direction of medical director, Dr. Steve Rasmussen, Dermatologist.

Check it out Saturday, April 19, at their Open House. Mini-demonstrations, giveaways, light refreshments! Free and open to everyone. 2 - 6pm 1717 West 6th St., Ste. 120R, in the Hartland Plaza (between West 5th & West 6th Sts.).

And, until April 30th, receive 20% off laser hair removal and $50 off wrinkle fillers Restylane and Juvederm. A new, improved you just might start here!
