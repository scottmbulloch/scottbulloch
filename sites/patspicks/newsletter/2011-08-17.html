More Frou Frou  
     
Frou Frou is a downtown salon that was recommended some time ago by one of the most beautiful women in Austin. They also carry top of the line skin and hair products. There is update: Frou Frou will be moving to a new location by September 5th. The space is in a more convenient area on West 6th Street with "more" available parking for customers! The studio will be located at 1504 West 6th St Austin, TX 78703.

    
Hat Creek  
     
I've been hearing so much about the Hat Creek Creek Burger Company, especially since they will be opening a new location at Bee Caves Road and 360. I went to their Burnet Road place and it's true the burgers are great. What I like was that you could get a "Little Hat" burger and mini shake, which is all I want a lot of time.

5K for brain cancer  
     
Sometime ago I mention Kelly Hoffman and the foundation she started to raise money for brain cancer research because of her experience with the disease after the birth of her first child. Saturday, September 10 the group will host a 5K run in Berry Springs Park & Preserve in Georgetown at 8:30am. Registration is now $25. Details are available here.
