Save Our Suit  
     
Patti Obenhaus passes along her suit tips from the Carlisle Collection. I know it's tempting to take off a "less-than-immaculate" suit and toss it in the dry cleaning pile but unless it is stained, it may only need to be steamed.

Save money and your suit! Ironing can flatten lapels and make some fabrics shiny. And don't forget to clean all pieces of the suit at the same time so they become worn at the same rate.
