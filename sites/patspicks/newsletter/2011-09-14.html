Fall Fest  
     
16 Texas wineries and 14 celebrity chefs will participate in two days jammed with food and wines. Events are at Horseshoe Bay and Marble Falls. This is the festival founded by Susan Auler, co-owner of Fall Creek Vineyards and founder of the Texas Hill Country Wine and Food Festival, so you know the participants will be the best. More info here.

September 30 - October 1, $25-$75 for individual events

    
And in San Antonio...  
     
And if that not enough for you, Texas wineries will be in San Antonio Wed and Thurs (Sept 28 & 29) of that week with a Road Show Trade and Media Tasting. The event begins with a winemaker dinner at Silo Elevated Cuisine. Details are not available yet.

Thurs night there will be a tasting for the public at the Pearl Studio (part of the old Pearl Brewery) with 17 participating wineries. $50 in advance ($55 at door, cash only) Details and tickets here.

While you're in San Antonio, you could try two of my favorite places Biga on the Banks and the Guenther House. The house was built in 1859 by the founder of one of the largest flour mills at the time. When I worked in San Antonio I loved to have breakfast at Guenther House. Plus you can buy the mixes to take home (but that seemed a little too much!)

The view of the Riverwalk makes meals at Biga even better. Check the menu here. It is fabulous! And the founder and top-rated chef, Chef Bruce Auden, has opened Auden's Kitchen in Stone Oak in north SA.
