Spice up your life (literally)!  
     
Penzeys, the spice store that sells spices I can't even pronounce much less know what they are, has come to Austin. Joyce Moeller has been to the Dallas store but not the Austin store so we can't tell you much about it other than it is at 4400 N. Lamar Ste. 103. But if you go their Web site at www.penzeys.com you will see the huge list of spices they carry. Let us know what you think!

Ombre has moved to nails  
     
If you have a hidden edgy side (or maybe not so hidden) this is for you. Nail polish colors have gone one step further. The polish comes in sets of five small bottles with a different shade of the same color (one shade for each finger). Nordstrom has three different sets. The black one, for example, starts with jet black and ends with silver!
