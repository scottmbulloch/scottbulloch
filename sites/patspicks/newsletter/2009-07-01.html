The "It" herb of summer  
     

Chives! The "You" doctors say it is packed with vitamin C, potassium, calcium, iron, and folic acid, Chives are used in many parts of the world to do more than spruce up the flavor of bland dishes. In traditional Chinese medicine, chives are used to relieve congestion, stomachaches, and even bad breath. Sprinkle them over salads or into stir-fries.


    
What? I have high blood pressure?  
     

During a routine check up my doctor noted that my blood pressure was on the high side. No medication yet, but definitely something to watch. That can't be; I've never had high blood pressure. Guess I do now.

Anyway, Bottom Line/HEALTH says it's not just sodium that you need to watch, but the excess of sodium in relation to potassium. You should eat three servings a day of high-potassium foods such as bananas, apricots, avocados, cantaloupe, figs, raisins, beans, potatoes, winter squash & unsalted tomato sauce.
