Did you see the would-be Hermes Birkin tote bag?
Several bloggers and the New York Times have been talking about a new bag by Thursday Friday that is a screen-printed tote that looks like the Hermes Birkin…but for $35! It's a shopping tote or can be substituted for your handbag. But if you want to order one, the red is won't ship until the end of February so you need to get on the wait list. Also in blue and tan.
Get ready to clear your clutter!
What a great way to get ready for spring. The Austin Chapter of the National Association of Professional Organizers will sponsor a day to help you get rid of all that stuff you don't know what to do with!

Saturday, January 29, 10am - 2pm
IKEA Parking Lots G & H
Drop off for FREE:
  • ONE Carload of unwanted items (junk)*
  • Computers and electronics
  • THREE boxes of paper to be shredded at off-site facility
  • Discarded flattened moving boxes
  • Used cell phones
  • Mobility assistance devices (wheelchairs, walkers, crutches, etc.)
Thanks to Amy Praskac, professional organizer and owner of On the Record

*Flammable, corrosive, toxic, poisonous, reactive, or explosive (including paint and batteries) cannot be accepted at this event.

More Vitamin D news
Here and here.

The YOU doctors (Dr. Oz) recommend checking your Vitamin D levels to protect your kidneys. I had heard that if you live in the south you probably got enough Vitamin D from sunshine. Maybe not. Different skin types utilize Vitamin D at different rates. I have been interested in Vitamin D because of its potential to discourage cancer cells. But you must check your levels with your doctor before starting supplements…don't want to get toxic! I'll keep you posted on my research.
