Eleanor
Eleanor Powell’s family considers her a hopeless arts and crafts addict. But, long ago they gave up trying to reform her. Eleanor loves art shows in the streets, in galleries, in metal sheds, or in home studios. For the past 10 years she has served as a director on the Artists Harvest Board of Directors. The art show has a 40-year history in Travis County. Discovering new outstanding talent is her favorite pastime. Welcome to the fun!
