Hopefully photos catch moments of beauty, humor, tradition, archaeology, architecture, detail, pattern, movement, reflection, uniqueness and similarity of people and places… the palette of the place. Equally important in my photography and travels are destinations which are central in the great trade routes of history. Along these paths not only commodities were transported, the sharing of knowledge, language, cuisine, arts, handicrafts, religion, and thought made their way through our world. As such, many of my most frequented areas are all of Asia, India, Central Asia and Turkey, North Africa and the Mediterranean Countries, South and Central America. And my guilty pleasure is beautiful water and beaches wherever they may exist.

  • Alaska, Northern Lights, ANWAR, polar bears and Copper River Delta
  • Antarctica, Falklands, South Georgia
  • Arizona always ablaze with color
  • China over all time zones, and several decades
  • Egypt, the Nile, the cities, oasis, and Western Desert
  • European Christmas Markets - a favorite new passion, Budapest, Vienna, Prague, Munich, Strasbourg and more - a winter wonderland of good feeling
  • Galapagos - a seal sunning on a dinghy and blue footed boobies dance
  • Guatemala - magnificent textile arts to majesty of Tikal
  • India - Ganges to the vibrant colors of Rajasthan
  • Italy - Venice in all seasons and the Riviera at its best
  • Khmer Kingdoms - the beauty of SE Asia
  • Mongolia - a magically untamed country
  • New Zealand
  • Other Americas - incomparable Cuba, the Caribbean, Patagonia
  • Peru - colonial Cusco, the gateway to Machu Pichu
  • Tibet, the Eastern Kham
  • Around the globe diversity reigns… Morocco, Vietnam, Cambodia, Tahiti and Central Asia
