With the advent of exceptional digital printmaking, I now have confindence that the highly color saturated images I love can carefully be rendered on prints. Further, the varieties of paper possible for printing include watercolor, cannvas and the total range of matte to high gloss. Each print is personally controlled by me insuring that the final image is truly reflective of the image you desire, and as such a truly individual piece. The only use of digital imaging programs such as Photoshop is to insure the highest quality rendering of the image, not changing it.

As such, prices are determined on factors such as the size desired, which fine art paper is chosen forthe outcome and if additional scanning is required. The most common sizes are 9x14 and 14x20 but many other sizes of course are available.

Customer satisfaction is absolute. All works shipped via Federal Express.
