News: Excellence in Academics Tutoring is proud to announce that it is using Susan Barton’s Reading and Spelling System for students—both for young students and adults—that struggle with Dyslexia. Susan Barton is renowned for her work in the field of Dyslexia. Her fail proof system will guarantee success for students who have difficulties reading. For more information about Susan Barton, please visit, www.bartonreading.com and www.brightsolutions.us

Excellence in Academics Tutoring, LLC. knows that in order for children to be fluent readers two things must happen - a reading inventory that is created by national reading experts must be given, and an evaluation must be written indicating the child’s weaknesses, and also, indicating what remediation techniques will be used to correct the weaknesses. After this is completed, the reading program focuses on:

  • Explicit and systematic instruction in phonological awareness
  • The skills of decoding
  • The skill of identifying derivational morphemes
  • The skills of seeing groups of words as “families”
  • The use of vocabulary from school and outside texts to increase the child’s mental lexicon
  • The practice of oral and silent reading
  • The practice of spelling, and linking it to the idea that each spelled word is pronounced a certain way, due to phonemic awareness.
  • Repetition of concepts that the student struggles with, in order to overcome it and achieve mastery, and also, to ensure that the process of transferring knowledge from short to long term memory happens.
