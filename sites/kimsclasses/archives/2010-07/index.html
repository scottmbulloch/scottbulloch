al Moro Fitness with Kim

This is a fast paced, 60-75 minute class that incorporates many different forms of exercise including: cardio, ballet barre, pilates, yoga, and resistance training with handweights. Al Moro Fitness is an action packed, upbeat, fun class for any level of fitness. Space for this class is limited due to equipment, so please call me to reserve your spot.

Cardio Sculpt

Cardio Sculpt is a high energy class that combines cardio, resistance training with hand weights, and plenty of ab work. This class is designed to be easy to follow and will sculpt and strengthen every major muscle group.

Mat Pilates & Cardio

Pilates exercise dramatically transforms the way your body looks, feels and performs. It builds strength without excess bulk, creating a sleek, toned body with slender thighs and a flat abdomen. A key component to the workout is the Pilates breath work, it increases lung capacity and creates a mind-body connection. Cardio exercises are sprinkled through this class to give you a well-rounded workout.

Vinyasa Pilates

Vinyasa yoga and mat pilates all in one class. The word Vinyasa means "breath-synchronized movement." In other words, I will instruct you to move from one pose to the next on an inhale or an exhale. This technique is flowing and with the smooth way that the poses run together, it becomes like a dance. I add standing and mat pilates to the flow to strengthen the core. Vinyasa Pilates is a yoga-based fitness class, so you get the best of both worlds. Reservations for this class are not required, so feel free to drop in!
