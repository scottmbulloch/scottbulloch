Hi, I'm Fred. I was born in Wilmington, Delaware a long, long time ago. I lived my early days in Chicago and Springfield, Illinois before moving to Scottsdale in 1964. I went to Scottsdale High, and immediately fell in love with Arizona like everyone else. In 1967, I joined the Marine Corps and proudly served 2 tours of duty in Vietnam, just south of DaNang at Marble Mountain. I made it home and found a variety of jobs to keep me happy. I have been very fortunate in always finding things that are interesting and enjoyable to me. Managing a sailboat shop and racing sailboats professionally for 6 years was one of them. I believe having fun and enjoying what you do is critical.

I got into the furniture business in the late 80's, working for a company in the Scottsdale airpark. I started my own furniture store as well, but found that I enjoy being on my own and helping designers with final touches (hanging art, mirrors, etc.). I love transporting things around the country for people and making them smile with my service.

My hobbies: avid motorcyclist, racquetball, sailing, cooking and being at home with my kids Daisy and Amy, Arf Arf!
