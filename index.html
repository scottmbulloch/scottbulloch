About Me

I pride myself in attention to detail, from the screen rendering to the code itself, everything must be clean and organized. My focus is making beautiful, usable and fun web apps. I enjoy developing micro-interactions and adding UI animations to the projects I work on. One of my strengths is the variety of work I have done and the ability to jump into different environments.

I have studied computer science but have a degree in design. I love the web because it a place where art and programming meet. My passion is finding ways to make the web a better place through code and an eye for detail. There is always something new to learn and being a web developer is always exciting.

A large focus of mine is responsive design and user experience. I am obessive about precision and proper execution but I enjoy breaking the boundaries of expectation and trying new things.
