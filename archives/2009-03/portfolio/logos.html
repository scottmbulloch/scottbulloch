Logos

Pat's Picks Austin

This logo was developed with the idea that several other regoinal divisions of the company would be added in the future. The brand is easily maintained and expanded by changing the location and keeping the rest of the logo intact.

Blacklight Photography

This is a redesigned logo for acclaimed world photographer Sally Ruscitti. Originally just the shutter/yin-yang, she requested it be reworked and expanded. I rebuilt and cleaned up the existing logo, then added the scroll banner and new text.

Art And Framing Designs

This logo was designed to replaced an older, outdated logo for an art gallery in Scottsdale. By using clean, bold lines it is now contemporary and relevant in the art world. The angled, rectangular background can also be changed to highlight featured works or incorporate new graphics for special events.

Wellness Works

Wellness works is a total health center involving physical therapy, massage, diet counseling, infrared skin treatment and more. The logo represents well-being, warmth and comfort.
